Consent & Disclosure for FTx Pulse

Before we proceed with module participation, please carefully read and acknowledge the following:

1. Purpose of Participating

You authorize sharing your store data with FasTraT POS LLC to enable FTx Pulse analytics and reporting that help you improve product setup and sales.

2. Information We Collect & Process

Product Catalog

We will access and process your product catalog information, including product names, descriptions, UPCs, categories, and pricing. This data will be compared against our Product Master Database (PMD) to identify matches and unique items. Unique products from your catalog may be copied and submitted for review to be potentially included in the PMD. You will earn credits for approved contributions.

Sales Data

We will collect and analyze your sales data, such as transaction dates, quantities sold, and sales trends. This information is crucial for generating insights, identifying best-selling products, and providing you with aggregated reports and benchmarks to help you grow your business.

3. How Your Information Will Be Used

  • To analyze your product catalog and sales data for accuracy and completeness.
  • To generate personalized dashboards, reports, and insights on your FTx Pulse account.
  • To enhance and expand our Product Master Database (PMD) with new product information.
  • To provide you with benchmarks and comparative analytics against anonymized, aggregated industry data.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

Your specific, identifiable data will not be shared with or sold to third parties without your explicit consent. We may use anonymized and aggregated data for industry reports, trend analysis, and marketing purposes. Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel of FasTraT POS LLC for the purposes outlined in this document.

5. Credits, Reviews, and Visibility

By contributing new products to the PMD, you are eligible to earn credits. The review and approval process for these contributions will be managed by our data team. Approved contributions and their statuses will be visible on your FTx Pulse Dashboard.

6. Security & Confidentiality

We are committed to protecting your data. We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits, to safeguard your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

7. Data Retention

Your data will be retained as long as you are an active participant in the FTx Pulse program. If you choose to opt-out, your personally identifiable data will be removed from our active systems in accordance with our data retention policy.

8. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, and maintained on, computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ from those from your jurisdiction.

9. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. You can also opt-out of the FTx Pulse program, which will cease any further data collection from your account. Please contact our support team to exercise these rights.

10. How we process the data?

Your data will be synchronized with FTX PULSE on a daily basis, according to the configured schedule. A scheduled cron job will perform the synchronization process by comparing your account data with the Master Database to ensure accuracy, completeness, and consistency. All updates and results from this synchronization will be reflected on your FTX PULSE Dashboard, where you can review the most recent insights and status changes. In case you wish to unsubscribe from FTX PULSE, you may simply disable the “Share Data” feature in your account settings. Once disabled, the synchronization process will stop automatically, and your account will be unsubscribed from the FTX PULSE module.

11. Authority to Share

By agreeing to this consent, you confirm that you are the rightful owner of the store data or have been granted the necessary authority by the owner to share this data with FasTraT POS LLC for the purposes described herein.

12. Changes to this Consent & Disclosure

We may update this Consent & Disclosure from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes and will require your renewed consent to continue your participation in the program.
